5n2 - 6n < 5n2 for n >= 1. So, 5n2 - 6n = O(n2). Also, 5n2 - 6n >= 5n2 - n2 = 4 n2 for n >= 6. So, 5n2 - 6n = Omega(n2). Consequently, 5n2 - 6n = Theta(n2).
